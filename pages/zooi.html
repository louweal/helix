Set an appropriate deposit amount for each product by considering various factors such as its materials, as well as other relevant factors related to the production and shipping process. Additionally, evaluate the anticipated lifespan of the product for an average customer.

To ensure transparency and provide valuable information to your customers regarding the environmental impact of your products, include both the deposit as the anticipated lifespan in the product details on your website.

Following each purchase, you can now offer your customers the opportunity to pay the designated deposit, as you specified earlier, using Helix.
